How to care?

Wash cold
Wash clothes in cold water, as opposed to warm or hot, and always wait until you have a full load. Hang or lay flat to dry.

Hand wash
For delicate items stick to spot cleaning only. Otherwise, handwash cold with a mild detergent to maintain the color and feel.

Steaming and ironing
Iron it on the lowest setting. Spray some water to help straighten out the wrinkles.
