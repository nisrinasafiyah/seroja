About us

Seroja brings you the difference between elegance in women. We BE DIFFERENCE is our slogan. We share the joy of giving the premium. With our superior craftsmanship, with high-quality fabric-made, we offer the best product for our customers. We are based in Malaysia and currently, we just opening a new branch outlet in Alor Setar, Kedah.

Vision

Our vision is to empower and inspire women around the world by providing high-quality hijab products that celebrate their individuality, enhance their style and promote cultural diversity.

Mission

Our mission is to create a hijab business that meets the unique needs and preferences of Muslim women while fostering inclusivity and embracing diverse fashion trends.
